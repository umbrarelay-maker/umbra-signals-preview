# NightOwl — Wireframes

## Design Philosophy
Umbra Archive aesthetic (JetBrains Mono, dark terminal UI, scanlines, paper grain) repurposed as a daily AI/tech intelligence digest. Umbra narrates current events as strategic journal entries.
